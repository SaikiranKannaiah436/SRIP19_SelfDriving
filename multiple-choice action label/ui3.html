    Imagine you are driving the car. Select all the actions that are possible to implement at the end of the video, and their reasons. Please follow the traffic rules, see more detail in Driver Handbook.

    Forward: Choose this action if the road condition is safe to drive forward. Remember to consider traffic lights, traffic signs and lane marks on the road.

  • Choose Traffic light is green or yellow if you are approaching to the intersection and traffic light is green or suddenly turns to yellow.
  • Choose Road clear if the road is quite empty and you can drive at your own speed.
  • Choose Follow traffic if you can maintan or increase current speed but have to pay attention to surrounding vehicles.
  • If there's green light and road is clear, choose both reasons.

    • Stop/Slow down: Choose this action if there're any traffic rules or obstacles on the road making you feel unsafe to maintain current speed. Otherwise, don't stop. Please observe the surrounding carefully, including stop sign and pedestrians, etc.

  • Please follow the traffic lights and traffic signs on the road, e.g. red light or stop sign, etc.
  • Choose Obstacles: xxx if it is unsafe to maintain current speed due to that object.
  • If there's red light and pedestrians are crossing in front you, choose both reasons.

    • Turn left/right: means make turn at intersection. Choose either or both if you are able to do so. Choose the reasons why you can turn left or right at the intersection.

  • Choose in the left/right lane when you are in the correct lane to make left/right turn.
  • Choose Traffic light or sign allow left/right turn if there's traffic light or sign specific for left/right turn.
  • Choose Front car turned left/right when you see the car in front you made the turn. If you cannot see whether you are in the right or left lane, just follow the front car. Usually, the leftmost and rightmost lanes are for turning.

    • Change lane to left/right: means you can change lane to left lane or right lane when there's enough space to your left or right to change lane. Also there should be dash line, don't cross solid line.

    Can't change lane: sometimes you can't change lane because of some reason: no lane to change, car occupying the lane or can't cross solid lane. If you can't change lane, choose one or more reasons that apply.

    Confuse: Choose this action when the given information in video is not clear or confusing, and you don't know how to act.

    Action: Forward, since you should follow traffic and traffic light is green or yellow;
    Action: Can't change lane to left, because no lane in the left;
    Action: Change lane to right, since no cars in the right lane and dashed line can be crossed.

    Wrong answer: Action: Stop/Slow down, Turn right.
    Correct answer:
    Action: Forward, because: follow traffic;
    Action: Can't change lane to left, since can't cross solid line;
    Action: Can't change lane to right, since can't cross solid line and cars in the right lane.
